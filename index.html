Welcome to CIP @ IITH

Center for Interdisciplinary Programs has been created with a vision of fostering interdisciplinary studies across various disciplines at IIT Hyderabad. We @ IIT Hyderabad envision to create new paradigms in education integrating techniques, tools and science from multi and cross disciplinary expertise on IITH campus.
