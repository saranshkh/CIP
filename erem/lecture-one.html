Industry Lecture Series

Why Industry lecture series?

What can be better than getting to know recent technological advances, industry practices, opportunities from the industry Experts themselves. Industry lectures are a first of its type initiative by IIT Hyderabad, a first step towards the vision of our Director sir to have more collaboration between the industry and academia. Industry veterans come forth and address the IIT Hyderabad community on the latest research, activities in industry, areas where more research is needed in industry point of view, students also interact with them asking their doubts and further understanding industry culture.

Title : Introduction to Supply Chain Optimization

Speaker : Dr. Kedar Kulkarni, Senior Manager, IBM Research

Abstract

This talk will focus on introducing the motivation for supply chain optimization, followed by an overview of the important technical problems and challenges that need to be addressed to design and operate modern supply chains efficiently. State-of-the-art approaches to address these problems will also be summarized. Sector-specific examples from the industry will be used to illustrate the complexity and importance of addressing these problems. In the later part of the talk, the importance of sustainability in supply chains will be highlighted. Time permitting, a quick walk-through of IBM's solutions in the supply chain area will also be carried out.

Speaker's Bio

Dr. Kedar Kulkarni obtained his B.Tech / M.Tech from IIT-Bombay, India (in 2003), and Ph.D. from the University of Illinois, USA (in 2007). He is currently a senior researcher and manager at IBM Research where he leads a global team of research scientists/software engineers focusing on the sustainability of supply chains. He has over 15 years of expertise on the broad/horizontal areas of mathematical modeling, optimization, and machine learning. Over the years, he has contributed to several business verticals: the chemical process industry, renewable energy, IT operational analytics, enterprise asset management/maintenance, to name a few.

He has over 40 international publications, including 15 filed-patents and 1 book to his credit. He has referred to many international journals/conferences of repute and has been invited for lectures/talks at various educational / research institutions/fora.

Title : Electronic - waste: Opportunities and Challenges

Speaker : Shri Ratnesh Rathi, Founder, and CEO – Green IT Recycling Center Pvt Ltd

Abstract & Bio

Ratnesh Rathi studied engineering as a graduate course and later in 1996 established a company in Pune of Refurbished IT assets by the name of Rajratan Sales Corporation. From the last 25 years of his experience, he has served and mentored many organizations. He was President of the Computer Media Dealers Association. He successfully ran an awareness campaign in Pune. But his concern towards the environment and its protection made him establish a new company – Green IT Recycling Center Pvt Ltd for e-waste recycling. As e-Waste itself is new in form, Green IT uses different techniques for handling and disposal of e-waste which will be discussed in this seminar.

Title : Opportunities present in the waste recycling sector for the young entrepreneurs

Speaker : Shri Akshay Jain, Managing Director – Namo eWaste Management Limited

Abstract

This lecture would be based on the opportunities present in the waste recycling sector for young entrepreneurs. How they can make a good living for themselves and at the same time work towards the betterment of society.

Speaker's Bio

Mr. Akshay Jain graduated as a mechanical engineer, he attained his master’s degree in business management from Greenwich University, London. He was inspired by the responsible recycling mechanism present in the UK and the dire need for such a system in our country for our sustainable growth. The idea got into the shape of Namo eWaste in 2014 and since then, with his enthusiasm at the young age of 28 years, he has taken big steps to reach the goal of building one of the best e-waste recycling facilities in India. Namo eWaste is the fastest growing e-waste management company in India working with many public and private sector companies. Akshay has various business interests in the scrap recycling field. He has achieved enormous recognition in the said field since 2015 and has reached an 8-figure income mark.

Achivements

Building a successful model for collection and recycling of e-waste, countering the problems like competition from the unorganized sector, reverse logistics cost, and lack of awareness within the stakeholders for such type of recycling. He has delivered a complete model for the channelization of waste through our various online and offline initiatives. His model includes both B2B and B2C collections of e-waste. Namo eWaste has been awarded as ‘Best Recycling Company of the Year’ at Green India Awards, New Delhi 2018, and 2nd Prize at ‘3R Excellence Award for E-Waste Recyclers In India’ at 8th Regional 3R Forum In Asia and the Pacific at Indore 2018.
